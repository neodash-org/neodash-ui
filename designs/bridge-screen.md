## Color Coding
- **Source Chain**: Purple gradient (#6366f1 to #8b5cf6)
- **Destination Chain**: Blue gradient (#3b82f6 to #1d4ed8)
- **Processing**: Orange (#f59e0b)
- **Success**: Green (#10b981)
- **Error**: Red (#ef4444)
- **Warning**: Yellow (#fbbf24)

## Responsive Design
- **Desktop**: 3-column layout with side panels
- **Tablet**: 2-column layout, stacked on smaller screens
- **Mobile**: Single column, collapsible sections
